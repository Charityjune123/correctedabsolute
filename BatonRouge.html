City Hall in Baton Rouge

Here are some key facts about the city:

  1. Population: Approximately 230,000 people (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.).
  2. Year of Incorporation: 1817 (City of Baton Rouge, n.d.).
  3. Region of the State: Southeast region
  4. Classification: Urban city
  5. Average Income Level As of recent data, the median household income in Baton Rouge is around 50,000, which is below the state average of approximately $55,000.
    (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.).
